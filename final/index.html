About Me

Hello! My name is Giana, and I am a Software Engineering Student at New England Institute of Technology. This past year I have gained experience in computer science programming languages including Python, C#, SQL, and HTML/CSS. I am a motivated programming student that hopes to obtain a software development position where I can utilize my learned experiences.

Additional Information about myself is, I am a Navy Veteran. I served active duty for 8 years as a Gas Turbines Systems Mechanic. I have always loved working with my hands and problem solving. During my time in the service, I gained experience in Data and Records Management. Learning about the various technical programs I soon realized I was ready for a change of course. When I separated, I decided to pursue a Software Engineering degree.
